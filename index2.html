BIET Campus Lost & Found System

• This portal helps BIET students recover lost belongings inside campus.

• Students can report lost items with image and contact details.

• Anyone who finds an item can upload it in the Found section.

• Owners can identify their item and contact the finder directly.

• This avoids crowding notice boards and saves time.

• Return confirmation ensures transparency and responsibility.

• Only BIET students are allowed to access this system.

• This improves campus trust, safety and cooperation.
